Why I Chose Dunkey

I chose videogamedunkey also known as, Dunkey, as my influential person as he was a very outstanding character within the YouTube scene during the 2015s. Instead of being a basic person who decided to be family friendly and make safe content, Dunkey decided to work on his own projects to show what he wanted to express to his YouTube audience.

Starting off small he was very open about his opinions and did not care about the hate or negative opinions of his videos which was a major inspiration to me, as understanding that was an intriguing lesson that helped me and others, to become your own person.

Another major reason for me choosing Dunkey over most other YouTubers is the fact that I have been a part of his journey for many years now, and watching him over the years is and was very inspiring, as seeing that he got wed, and even had a child was surprising considering most content on social medias feel so fake. Despite a lot of backlash and distaste from his YouTube audience, Dunkey was still inspired to create and show that he is a different type of creator who loves to be genuine online even if his jokes are mostly satire.
